Frequently Asked Questions

Account Management
To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email.
Go to Profile & Settings from the sidebar menu, then click on "Personal Information" to update your details.
Navigate to Profile & Settings, then select "Notification Settings" to customize how and when you receive notifications.
Appointments
Go to the Appointments page and click "Book New Appointment". Select your doctor, preferred date and time, then confirm your booking.
Yes, you can reschedule appointments up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Click on the "Reschedule" button on your appointment card.
You can cancel appointments through the Appointments page. Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance to avoid cancellation fees.
Medical Records
Navigate to Medical Records, click on "Upload Documents", select your files (PDF, JPG, PNG supported), and add relevant descriptions.
We support PDF, JPG, PNG, and DOCX formats. Files should be under 10MB in size for optimal uploading.
Select the documents you want to share, click "Share", choose your doctor from the list, and add any relevant notes.
